Kent Nguyen

Hi there! I'm Kent Nguyen, your go-to guy when it comes to tech startups and innovative technology. Since 2023, I've been proudly leading Alternō as the CEO, founder, and inventor of the sand battery technology. Simultaneously, I also hold the chair at Alpha Bits. My adventure started with Anatics, where I served as a founder and technologist from 2020 to 2023. Soon after, I took the tech lead at OP3N LLC and Triip.me, adding a wealth of experience to my journey. My passion for startups led me to join Viet Capital Ventures as a venture partner from 2018 to 2021. An unforgettable chapter of my life was my stint as Head of Engineering for Grab in 2017. Earlier, I co-founded Silicon Straits and TGM Corporation. Every step of this journey has shaped the entrepreneur in me, and I can't wait to see what the future holds for us at Alternō!
